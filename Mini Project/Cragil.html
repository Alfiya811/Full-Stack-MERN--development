What We Provide

Comprehensive Planning: We work closely with you to understand your goals and the needs of your beneficiaries. Our team helps you design a trust structure that provides for your loved ones and reflects your intentions.
Customization: Every trust is unique. We tailor the terms and conditions of your trust to meet your specific objectives, whether it’s for asset protection, estate planning, or managing wealth.
Asset Allocation: We assist in identifying and transferring assets into the trust, ensuring a smooth and efficient process.
Legal Expertise: Our experienced professionals ensure that your trust is compliant with all relevant legal requirements, including the Indian Trust Act, 1882. We handle the drafting of trust documents, ensuring clarity and precision.
Peace of Mind: By creating a trust with Cragil, you can be confident that your assets will be managed according to your wishes, providing security for your beneficiaries.
